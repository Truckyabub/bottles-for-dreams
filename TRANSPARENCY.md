# Transparency

This site is a preview for the Ninja Park community project. Transparency is a priority at every step.

## What we share

- This website does not collect personal information.
- This website does not process payments or store donor credit card details.
- Bottle drive and support details are shared only after they are confirmed.
- Funding and support are intended for planning, site preparation, safety review, and future community benefit.
- No registered charity status or tax receipt eligibility is claimed unless official documentation is confirmed.

## How support is tracked

- Bottle return proceeds are tracked through the Sunset Shores collection bins once the locations are confirmed.
- Private support is documented by the Ninja Park community organizers in a responsible, transparent way.
- All contributions are handled through trusted local channels, not through this website.

## Commitment

- Donation language is honest, grateful, and non-manipulative.
- No hardship, identity, culture, or child story is used as pressure.
- Sponsor interest is shared only with actual commitment or confirmation.
- Updates are posted when real details are available.
