# Child Safety and Ethics

Ninja Park is built around the principle that every child matters. Safety, dignity, privacy, and inclusion are essential.

## Child safety commitments

- The site does not collect children’s personal information.
- There are no child-facing forms or sign-up fields.
- All contact and support instructions are aimed at adults and guardians.
- Children’s photos, stories, and participation require parent or guardian consent.
- Volunteer language emphasizes supervision, privacy, safety rules, and reporting concerns.

## Ethical behaviour

- The site does not exploit children’s hardship, identity, culture, or images.
- Support messaging is honest, transparent, and thankful.
- No guilt, shame, or pressure is used to encourage giving.
- No safety or opening claims are made until approvals are in place.

## Project status

- Ninja Park is not currently open, approved, certified, or ready for public use.
- The zipline is a proposed future attraction, not an approved feature.
- Professional design, installation, inspection, insurance, permits, and accessibility review are required before use.
