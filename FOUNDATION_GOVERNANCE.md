# Foundation Governance

This document explains the governance and accountability expectations for Ninja Park.

## Governance principles

- The project is guided by transparency, ethics, and community trust.
- Decisions are made with care for children’s safety, cultural respect, and long-term inclusion.
- Financial accountability is maintained through clear tracking of support and expenditures.
- Official roles, responsibilities, and reporting are confirmed by the organizer group.

## Real-world confirmations required

- Formal organizer authorization is confirmed before launch.
- Registered charity or nonprofit status is confirmed before any claim is made.
- Tax receipt eligibility is confirmed only with legal documentation.
- Donation accounts and payment details are verified before public sharing.
- Land approval, permits, insurance, and professional oversight are confirmed before opening.
- Zipline design, installation, inspection, maintenance, accessibility, and safety plans are all confirmed before use.
- Indigenous and cultural guidance is confirmed with the appropriate local communities.
