# Cultural Respect and Humility

Ninja Park is committed to respecting Indigenous peoples, Métis communities, Inuit communities, and all cultures.

## Cultural humility

- "Every child matters" is treated respectfully and seriously.
- No Indigenous symbols, artwork, storytelling, or Nation-specific cultural elements are used without permission.
- No generic pan-Indigenous design is used.
- No culture is used as decoration or marketing.
- No culture is tokenized, stereotyped, or used as emotional leverage.

## Guidance and consent

- Formal land acknowledgements or cultural statements require guidance from the appropriate local Indigenous Nations, Métis representatives, Inuit representatives, Elders, Knowledge Keepers, families, and community members.
- The site does not claim to represent, speak for, or partner with any Nation, Band, school, charity, organization, or community unless confirmed.
- Any future cultural work is undertaken with consent, respect, and proper input.
