# Privacy and Security

This website is built for transparency, privacy, and community trust. It does not collect personal information or process payments.

## Privacy principles

- No analytics or visitor tracking is included.
- No cookies are used for tracking or advertising.
- No personal information is collected through forms or on-site donation processing.
- Contact fields are placeholders until organizer details are verified.

## Donation handling

- The site does not accept credit card payments directly.
- Support may be coordinated privately through e-transfer, cash, sponsorships, volunteer help, materials, or community sharing.
- Private support details should be handled outside the website in a secure, trusted manner.

## Security notes

- External links are opened securely when present.
- The site is static HTML, CSS, and JavaScript with no backend.
- If hosted publicly, confirm HTTPS support.

## Publication guidance

- Keep the `noindex, nofollow` robots directive until all Ninja Park details are confirmed.
- Change to `index, follow` only when the campaign is ready for public launch.
- Verify the final copy before publishing.
