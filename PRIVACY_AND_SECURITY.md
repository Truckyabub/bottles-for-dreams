# Privacy and Security

This website is designed for safety, privacy, and trust. It does not collect personal data or process payments for Ninja Park support.

## Privacy principles

- No analytics or visitor tracking is included.
- No cookies are used for tracking or advertising.
- No personal information is collected through forms or on-site donation processing.
- Contact details are placeholders until verified by Ninja Park organizers.

## Donation handling

- The site does not accept credit card payments directly.
- Support may be offered privately through e-transfer, cash, sponsorship, volunteer help, materials, or community sharing.
- Any private support details should be handled outside the website in a secure, trusted manner.

## Security notes

- External links are set to open securely in new windows.
- The site uses simple static HTML, CSS, and JavaScript with no backend.
- If the site is published, make sure the hosting provider supports HTTPS.

## Publication guidance

- Keep the `noindex, nofollow` robots directive in place until all Ninja Park project details are confirmed.
- Only update the site to `index, follow` when the campaign is ready for public launch.
- Confirm the Ninja Park organizer approves the final copy and public details.
