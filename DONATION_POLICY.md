# Donation Policy

Ninja Park is a community-driven preview project. This document explains the ethical donation approach.

## Donation principles

- Donations are voluntary and appreciated.
- The site does not yet accept payments directly.
- Donation guidance is adult-facing and designed for trusted local coordination.
- No child personal information is requested or collected.
- No tax receipt is promised unless registered charity status is confirmed.

## Intended use of support

Support is intended for:

- planning and design work for the park and zipline concept
- safety review, certification, insurance, and permit planning
- equipment, materials, and site preparation
- accessibility improvements and safe surfacing
- volunteer coordination, supervision, and respectful outreach
- future maintenance and care of the shared space

## Responsible fundraising

- Donation details are marked "Coming Soon" until they are verified.
- Any fundraising program is disclosed clearly and honestly.
- The site avoids exaggerated progress or fake milestones.
- The community retains accountability for how funds are used.
