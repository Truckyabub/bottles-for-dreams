Vision

Our vision is bigger than a bottle drive.

“Our vision is to turn everyday bottles into extraordinary possibilities. Through community bottle collection in Fort McMurray, we are helping create an outdoor Ninja Park wonderland for children in a smaller rural community where recreation funding is limited and youth need more places to play, grow, belong, and dream. This park will be more than equipment — it will be a place of adventure, confidence, friendship, laughter, fresh air, and community pride. Every bottle collected helps clean up our surroundings, lift up young hearts, and bring one more miracle closer to reality.”

Community-driven

Bottle Drive

How the fundraiser works

Bottles are collected in Fort McMurray.

Community members save recyclable bottles that can be cleaned up and returned together.

The goal supports children in a smaller rural community.

The fundraiser is built to help bring a safe, active outdoor Ninja Park closer to reality.

Every bottle helps two ways.

It keeps recyclables out of parks, roadsides, and shared spaces while building toward something meaningful.

It is about belonging, adventure, and opportunity.

The project is designed to help kids feel active, included, and excited about what their community can create.
