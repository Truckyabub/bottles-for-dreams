About the fundraiser

Community-powered recycling with a playful twist

Our bottle drive provides a safe, privacy-preserving space to learn how recycling supports local parks and youth programs. The site is fully static, accessible, and built for a quick publish to GitHub Pages from the main branch.

No tracking

We keep this site free of analytics, external tracking, and server-side forms.

Mobile friendly

Responsive design ensures the page is easy to browse on phones and tablets.

Three built-in games

Play games that teach recycling and support the fundraiser with interactive fun.
