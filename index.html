Community Bottle Drive Fundraiser

Bottles for Dreams

Helping collect bottles, clean up parks, and build a dream space where children, families, neighbours, puppies, laughter, and community joy can grow together.

How It Works Get Involved

Collect

Gather refundable bottles and cans from homes, workplaces, events, and neighbours who want to help the community.

Recycle

Turn bottles into clean parks, less waste, and real fundraiser value for a brighter shared future.

Build Dreams

Every bag, bin, and bottle brings the dream park one step closer for children and families.

Ways to Help

  • Save your bottles and cans.
  • Invite friends, family, and coworkers.
  • Share the campaign with your community.
  • Volunteer for pickup or sorting days.

Our Spirit

Live, love, laugh, and keep bottles from flooding the parks. This fundraiser is built on kindness, teamwork, and big smiles.
