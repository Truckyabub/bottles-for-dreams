Mission

Build a safe, playful place where youth can belong, challenge themselves, and feel proud of home.

Our Ninja Park bottle drive is about more than recyclables. It is a rural community coming together to give children a bright, hopeful outdoor space for climbing, laughing, exploring, and building confidence. Every bottle donated at Sunset Shores and every private contribution supports the journey toward a youth Ninja Park that belongs to everyone.

Where the Support Goes

Funds and contributions are tracked and used for the Ninja Park project.

Bottle returns and private donations are directed toward planning, materials, permits if needed, equipment, installation, safety features, signage, and future maintenance. Our goal is to keep the process honest, clear, and rooted in community trust.

Transparency

Tracking the support with care.

We commit to tracking funds and contributions clearly. Updates, milestones, and reporting will be shared as the Ninja Park project progresses. This is a community fundraiser, and every contribution is private and appreciated.

  • Collected bottle returns support Ninja Park costs.
  • Private contributions support planning, construction, and safety.
  • No charitable tax receipts are issued unless registered charity status is confirmed later.
  • Details will be updated openly as the campaign develops.
Community Vision

Building a place where kids can run, climb, laugh, and feel connected to their community.

Imagine an outdoor Ninja Park where children chase sunsets, practice new skills, cheer on friends, and discover the joy of shared play. This is a place for families to gather, volunteers to help, and the next generation to feel proud of what their community created together.
