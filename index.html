Mission

Build a youth-focused outdoor Ninja Park that gives kids a place to climb, play, grow confidence, and feel proud of their rural community.

Our Ninja Park bottle drive is designed to bring families, neighbours, and local supporters together for a brighter future. The park will offer a safe place for children to be active, connect with friends, and enjoy outdoor play close to home.
