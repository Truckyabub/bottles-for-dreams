# Donation Transparency

Ninja Park is a community fundraiser and local support campaign for a youth Ninja Park. All funds and contributions are tracked carefully and used to support the project goals.

## What is tracked

- Bottle return proceeds collected through the Sunset Shores donation bins.
- Private support received by e-transfer, cash donation, sponsorship, materials, volunteer help, or other private contribution.
- Any project costs that are paid with Ninja Park fundraiser support.

## How funds are used

Contributions are intended to be used for:

- Planning and design of Ninja Park
- Materials and equipment
- Permits or approvals if needed
- Installation, construction, and site preparation
- Safety surfacing and protective features
- Signage, community outreach, and volunteer coordination
- Future maintenance and care for the park

## Commitment to honesty

- We do not claim registered charity status unless official registration information is confirmed.
- Charitable tax receipts are not available unless Ninja Park later obtains confirmed charity status.
- This website does not process payments or collect credit card information directly.

## Notes for supporters

- Bottle donations can be dropped at the Sunset Shores bins.
- Private contributions are welcomed by contacting the organizer.
- Supporting the project is voluntary and appreciated.
- Updates will be shared as the Ninja Park project progresses and details are verified.
