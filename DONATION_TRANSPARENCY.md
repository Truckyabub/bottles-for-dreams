# Donation Transparency

Ninja Park is a community fundraiser and local bottle drive project. All support and contributions are tracked carefully and used to help build a youth-friendly outdoor play space.

## What is tracked

- Bottle return proceeds collected through the Sunset Shores donation bins.
- Private support received by e-transfer, cash donation, sponsorship, materials, volunteer help, or other private contribution.
- Project costs paid from Ninja Park community support.

## How funds and support may be used

- Planning and design for the Ninja Park project.
- Materials and equipment.
- Installation and site preparation.
- Safety surfacing, signage, and protective features.
- Permits or approvals if required.
- Volunteer coordination and community outreach.
- Future maintenance and care.

## Honesty and transparency

- Ninja Park does not claim registered charity status unless official registration is confirmed.
- Charitable tax receipts are not issued unless charity status is verified.
- This website does not process payments or collect credit card information directly.
- Funding updates should be shared publicly when details are verified.

## Notes for supporters

- Bottle donations can be dropped at the Sunset Shores bins.
- Private contributions are welcomed by contacting the organizer.
- Supporting this project is voluntary and appreciated.
- Information is shared openly as the Ninja Park project progresses.
