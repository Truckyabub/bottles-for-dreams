# Content Guide

Use this guide to help family members add or update content safely.

## Family Story Prompts

- What made Pa George special to the family?
- What did he enjoy about pool, community, or gatherings?
- What small habit or saying do you remember most?
- What would he want people to remember about him?

## Memory Wall Prompts

- A time Pa made everyone laugh
- A lesson Pa taught without trying
- A moment at a family dinner or gathering
- A story from the pool hall that still gets told

## Pool Hall Memory Prompts

- Who was there?
- What was the match or moment?
- What made the memory meaningful?
- Why does it matter to the family?

## Family Approval Checklist

- The wording is accurate
- The family has approved the content
- No private information is exposed
- No unverified claims are published
- Photo permissions are clear
- Sponsor names are approved
- Charity details are confirmed
- The tone feels respectful and warm
