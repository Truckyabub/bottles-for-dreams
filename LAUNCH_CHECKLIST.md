# Launch Checklist

Before public launch, confirm each of the following:

1. Confirm charity/fundraiser permission.
2. Confirm bottle drop-off details.
3. Confirm responsible organizer contact.
4. Confirm use of images and graphics.
5. Confirm donation/payment setup before adding any payment links.
6. Test mobile layout.
7. Test games.
8. Test GitHub Pages publishing.

## Notes

- Keep the site static until all approvals are complete.
- Do not add personal data collection unless the privacy and security implications are reviewed first.
- Keep any "Coming Soon" sections clearly labeled until the information is ready.
